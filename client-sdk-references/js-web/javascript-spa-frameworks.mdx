---

For [React](/client-sdk-references/js-web/javascript-spa-frameworks#react-hooks) and [Vue](/client-sdk-references/js-web/javascript-spa-frameworks#vue-composables) specifically, wrapper packages are available to support reactivity/live queries, which makes it easier for developers to leverage PowerSync. This doesnâ€™t mean that web projects with other frameworks cannot use PowerSync, but that the barrier to entry is a little higher than using such wrappers.

**Note**: Reactivity wrappers for other frameworks such as [Svelte](https://svelte.dev/) and [Solid](https://www.solidjs.com/) are planned further down the line. Please [let us know](/resources/contact-us) if you require this for your project.
